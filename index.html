ABOUT ME

I’m Chika, and I make art, super original, I know.

When I started everything felt synonymous... now I enjoy the process more than the end product. I like the flow. It’s what I want to see in the world. I hope you enjoy browsing.

Everything is moving fast and I’m glad to be able to pause for a second. I hope you find something that makes you smile. Chika is art, art is Chika. Keep your dreams alive, always find inspiration.

I enjoy textures, different styles of art, expressions in many colors, and the freedom to create our own stories. Grab a print and own a bit of heart in your room.
