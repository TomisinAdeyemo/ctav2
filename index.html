ABOUT ME

“Art is how I express
what I don’t have words for — yet.”

I’m Chiza, and I make art,
super original — I know.

Since I can remember, creativity has always been something that drove me. In school, it was the thing I looked forward to — whether it showed up as singing or sketching.

I started drawing early on, and I knew almost immediately that it would become an escape for me. Crochet, sketching, experimenting — each became a different mode of expression.

Over time, those expressions turned into evolving styles, many opinions, and the freedom to create pieces no one asked for — and now, I get to share that with you.
